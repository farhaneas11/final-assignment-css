ABOUT US

Chaithanya Granite & Marbles

CHAITHANYA GRANITE & MARBLE, is one of Kerala’s most highly regarded suppliers of granite, marble, engineered quartz surfaces, porcelain tiles and sanitary ware. Our company has been an indispensable tool to Builders, Architects and Designers for over three decades. Established in 1983 we are one of the initial dealers of granite and marble in Kerala.
